Directions

LASA (Liberal Arts and Science Academy of Austin) is located in east Austin near the intersection of Highway 290 and Highway 183.

From HWY 183 take Springdale Road, go east. Turn right on to Pecan Brook and follow Pecan Brook past the Delco center and a couple of blocks to Lazy Creek. LASA will be on the left.

From Hwy 290, turn on to Springdale Road, southwest, or Tuscany Road southwest and continue to the light at Pecan Brook. Take a left turn and continue on Pecan Brook past the Delco center and a couple of neighborhood blocks to Lazy Creek. LASA will be on the left.
